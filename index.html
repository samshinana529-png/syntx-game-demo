How to Play

Welcome to Crown Quest! Follow these rules to win the throne:

  1. Choose your total player slots and how many human players you want.
  2. Remaining slots will be filled by computer opponents, and you can choose their difficulty.
  3. Press Start Game to begin your adventure.
  4. If your token is still in base, roll the die until you get a 6 to leave base.
  5. Once you are on the board, move your token forward by the number rolled.
  6. After each move, answer the trivia question for the tile color you land on.
  7. If your roll is 1–3 and you answer correctly, you stay safe. If you answer wrong, move back one tile.
  8. If your roll is 4–6 and you answer correctly, advance one extra tile. If you answer wrong, stay where you are.
  9. The first player to reach the center WINNER tile wins the game.

Use the Restart Game button after a win, or return home to choose new player names.
